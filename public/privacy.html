GO IRL

Privacy Policy

Effective: 14 July 2026

What GO IRL does

GO IRL helps people discover, create, share, and join local real-life events. The service can be accessed through Telegram and supported Meta messaging channels.

Information we process

  • Platform identifiers and basic profile information supplied by Telegram, WhatsApp, Instagram, or Messenger.
  • Event, participation, and join-request information needed to operate the service.
  • Messages and button actions sent to GO IRL accounts when they are needed to process an event request.
  • Technical and security data needed to validate requests, prevent duplicate actions, and investigate failures or abuse.

GO IRL does not sell personal information or use messaging data for unrelated advertising.

Why we process it

We process this information to provide the requested event experience, keep events and participants consistent across supported channels, protect the service, and comply with applicable law. Processing is based on providing the service you request, legitimate security and operational interests, consent where required, and legal obligations.

Sharing and service providers

Information is shared only as needed with infrastructure and messaging providers that operate GO IRL, including Telegram, Meta platforms, Vercel, and Supabase. Event details you choose to publish may be visible to other event participants or users according to the event's visibility settings.

Retention and security

We keep information only for as long as needed to run events, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and protect the service. Access credentials remain server-side. We use request-signature checks, access controls, and duplicate-action protection where supported.

Your choices and rights

You may stop messaging GO IRL at any time. You may also request access, correction, export, restriction, objection, or deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable law. To request deletion or ask a privacy question, contact GO IRL through @GOirl_bot on Telegram. Include the messaging channel you used and enough information to locate your account; do not send passwords or access tokens.

Children

GO IRL is not intended for children under the minimum age required to use the relevant messaging platform or provide valid consent in their country.

Updates

We may update this policy as the service changes. The effective date at the top identifies the current version.
